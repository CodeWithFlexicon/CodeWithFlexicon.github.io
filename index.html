About Me

Hello there!

I'm Felix, a graduating senior in December 2024 majoring in Computer Science at CUNY College of Staten Island. My journey into programming began with my best friend trying to explain what a for loop was. Intrigued, I dove deeper, studying CS50 which led to me developing a deeper curiosity and later enrolling at CSI.

I am currently focusing on diving into new technologies for a random project I have in mind which might deal with APIs, databases, and a bit of frontend but I am mostly interested in backend engineering.

My ultimate aspiration is to collaborate with other passionate Software Engineers in developing scalable and maintainable products that have a positive impact on the lives of people around us.
